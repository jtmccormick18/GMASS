Valuation Services

GMASS began on the idea of a revaluation and staffing company. While our services may have expanded, that vision still stands true to this day. We offer a wide range of valuation services that covers a need for an extra appraiser during busy season all the way to full blown county-wide revaluations.

  • Residential
    Residential Appraisals are our bread and butter. We have a demonstrated history of success in providing reliable values and keeping your county in compliance with industry standards.
  • Commercial and Industrial
    The GMASS commercial team is comprised of the best in the business. We know income producing properties can provide more headache than residential. Our staff is experienced and equipped to help meet these challenges head on.
  • Rent-An-Appraiser
    Sometimes you just need an extra hand in your day to day office duties. We offer a variety of staffing resources to help meet your needs in any way we can.
